Tudor’s Black Bay

Luxury Swiss watch manufacturer Tudor has been cooking up something special. The respected company just announced the release of its Black Bay Ceramic dive watch. This might not seem that exciting, but for watch fans, it’s the technical side of things that makes this timepiece stand out.While the matte black ceramic finish, 41 mm case, and all-new, all-black MT5602-1U automatic in-house movement make this a fantastic addition to any watch collection, it’s the words “Master Chronometer” that catch your attention
