Belt

Belts may not be as flashy as a watch or necklace, but they are the most practical of men’s accessories. Whether you’re using one because the new pair of pants you bought turned out to be one size too big or you just lost a bunch of weight recently, a belt can save your outfit. But a belt doesn’t have to be all about practicality.The fashion industry has turned belts into one of the major highlights of any outfit. The right belt can turn a boring polo shirt and slacks combo into a casual-professional getup.
