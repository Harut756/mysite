Sunglasses

Sunglasses are the quintessential accessory, something worn all over the world by men and women alike. They’re pretty clever little inventions, too: they go with literally everything, can be worn any time of year, they’re readily available, and they’re offered at every possible price point. Many people don’t give much thought to their sunglasses. After all, it’s simple to walk into a shop, try a few pairs on, and walk out with them, right? Sure, but there’s actually more to them than very literally protecting your eyes.
